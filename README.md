# Glow-Up-Case-Study
Glow Up Campaigns ROI & Analytics Dashboard
Prepared by – Sagar Sharma
Introduction

Glow Up runs multiple marketing campaigns across digital platforms.
This report presents the performance of campaigns based on spend, leads, conversions, revenue,
and engagement.

Goal: Improve marketing performance and optimize ROI.

Dataset Overview
Dataset contains the following fields:
- Campaign Name
- Spend
- Leads
- Conversions
- Revenue
- ROI (%)
- CPA
- Clicks & Impressions
- Start Date, End Date
- Month
The dataset was cleaned, missing values were checked, and months were arranged properly.

Key Metrics (KPIs)
The dashboard highlights these main KPIs:
- Total Spend: Total money invested in campaigns
- Total Leads: Total interested customers
- Total Conversions: Customers who made purchases
- Total Revenue: Money earned from campaigns
- ROI (%): (Revenue – Spend) / Spend × 100
- CPA: Spend ÷ Conversions
  
Monthly Leads Trend
Month-wise analysis shows how leads changed throughout the year.
It helps identify:
- High-performing months
- Months with fewer leads
- Seasonal behavior
This trend supports planning future campaigns.

Campaign Performance Analysis
Campaign-wise performance includes:
- Spend comparison
- Leads generated
- Conversions achieved
- Revenue returned
- ROI percentage
Insights:
- Some campaigns deliver high conversions at low cost
- Some campaigns need optimization due to low returns
  
Revenue & Spend Comparison
A comparison between revenue and spend shows profitability.
Key observations:
- High revenue campaigns indicate strong targeting
- High spend but low revenue campaigns need improvement
- Balanced spend-to-revenue campaigns are ideal
  
Engagement Metrics
Engagement is measured using Clicks and Impressions.
Observations:
- High impressions but low clicks: Ads not attractive
- High clicks + high conversions: Strong campaign
- Missing engagement values indicate faulty tracking
  
Use of Filters (Slicers)
The dashboard includes slicers for:
- Month
- Campaign Name
- Channel
These allow users to:
- Analyze any single campaign
- Compare performance across months
- Check channel effectiveness
  
Findings
Main findings from the dashboard:
- Performance varies across campaigns
- Some months have significantly better leads
- CPA is high in some campaigns
- Engagement levels vary widely between channels
  
Recommendations
Based on analysis:
- Increase spending on high-performing campaigns
- Redesign or stop low-ROI campaigns
- Improve ad creatives for better engagement
- Focus on months with low leads using stronger offers
- Ensure correct tracking for clicks and impressions
